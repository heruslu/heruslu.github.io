About

Fourth year graduate student in Department of Mathematical Sciences, University of Delaware. I am currently in sustaining status which means I have completed all my course work, passed both of the preliminary tests and oral candidacy exam, and focusing on my thesis.

I am a proud member of Team Pancho supervised by Dr. Francisco Sayas. Other members of our team are: Allan Hungria, Shukai Du and Hugo Diaz.

Interests

Numerical Analysis, Finite and Boundary Element Methods, Continuous and Discontinuous Galerkin Methods, Elliptic PDEs, Viscoelasticity, Scattering Theory, Image Segmentation, High Performance Computing, Software and Algorithm Development.

Programming Interests

Python, MATLAB, NumPy/SciPy, FEniCS/DOLFIN, Object Oriented Programming, OpenMP, MPI, GitHub.
